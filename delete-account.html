Receipt Scan

Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Receipt Scan account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team from the email address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address in the message body.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Send your deletion request to:

support@trioitco.co.za

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All receipt images stored in Azure Blob Storage
  • All extracted receipt data (merchant names, amounts, dates)
  • Your subscription and billing history records held by us

What Is Retained

  • Google Play manages subscription billing records independently — contact Google Play for those.
  • Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics that cannot be linked back to you.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your receipts will be lost. We recommend exporting your data as CSV from within the app before requesting deletion.

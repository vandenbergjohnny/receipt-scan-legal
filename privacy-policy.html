Receipt Scan

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Receipt Scan ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and password hash (never stored in plain text).
  • Receipt images: uploaded to secure Azure Blob Storage linked to your account.
  • Extracted receipt data: merchant name, amounts, dates — stored in our database.
  • No payment card data is ever collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the receipt scanning, storage and export service.
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset). We do not send marketing emails.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

  • Images are stored on Microsoft Azure (South Africa North region).
  • Database is hosted on Microsoft Azure SQL.
  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

4. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • You may delete any receipt at any time from within the app.
  • To delete your account and all associated data, contact support@trioitco.co.za.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Microsoft Azure — storage and OCR processing.
  • Resend — transactional email only (resend.com).

6. Your Rights

  • You may request a copy of your data at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all data.
  • Contact: support@trioitco.co.za

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes are made.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: support@trioitco.co.za
